Exude effortless sophistication with the Soleil Mini, a masterpiece of modern tailoring and feminine draping. Designed to command attention from every angle, this dress blends a sharp, structured silhouette with soft, romantic accents.

Design Details:
- Avant-Garde Neckline: A striking one-shoulder silhouette featuring a high, asymmetrical neckline that flows seamlessly into the bodice.
- Signature Draping: The right shoulder is defined by a dramatic, oversized bow-tie element that drapes elegantly down the arm, creating a focal point of movement and texture.
- Tulip Silhouette: The waist is cinched to perfection, leading into a flared tulip skirt that offers a structured, voluminous shape while remaining incredibly flattering.
- Precision Tailoring: Built-in contour boning through the bodice provides a secure, hourglass fit without sacrificing comfort.
- Clean Finish: The back features a minimalist aesthetic with a hidden invisible zipper closure and vertical princess seams for a streamlined look.

Fabric & Composition:
Material: Premium weighted Satin Crepe — chosen for its matte-luxe finish and its ability to hold the architectural shape of the skirt.
Lining: Fully lined with a tonal, breathable silk-blend lining for a smooth feel against the skin.
Color: Radiant Lemon Sorbet.

Fit & Measurements (Based on Sample Size S):
- Length: Approximately 85cm from nape to hem (designed to hit at the mid-thigh).
- Fit: Structured bodice with no stretch; fits true to size.
- Sleeve Draping: 35cm unknotted length.

Styling Suggestions:
For a high-fashion editorial look, pair the Soleil with minimalist white pointed-toe heels and slicked-back hair to let the neckline shine. For an evening event, opt for metallic gold accessories and a bold red lip to contrast the vibrant yellow hue.
